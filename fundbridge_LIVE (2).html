FAQ
Every Question, Answered.
How does FundBridge verify investors?
+
Every investor profile is manually reviewed — PAN, Aadhaar, LinkedIn, investment history verified by our team before going live. You'll see a ✓ Verified badge on approved profiles.
Is real-time chat really private?
+
Yes. All messages use Supabase's secure infrastructure with Row-Level Security. Only the sender and recipient can read messages. Admin sees only metadata (who chatted with whom).
What is Supabase — is it really free?
+
Supabase is an open-source Firebase alternative backed by Y Combinator. Free tier: unlimited database rows, 50,000 monthly active users, 1GB storage, real-time subscriptions. No credit card, no expiry, truly free forever.
What is the Nexus AI bot?
+
Nexus is your 24/7 AI advisor powered by Claude (Anthropic). It's trained on Indian startup law, fundraising strategy, valuation methods, term sheet analysis, and pitch deck feedback. Ask it anything.
How long does it take to get funded?
+
Our average from listing to first investor commitment is 9 days. Full round closure averages 14-28 days. AI matching removes 90% of traditional friction.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
+
Yes. No lock-ins, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your dashboard anytime. Full refund within 7 days of first payment — no questions asked.
